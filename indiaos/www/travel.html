Your guide to Bangalore City

Having evolved gradually from being the Garden City to the Silicon Valley of India, Bengaluru is the third largest and one of the most livable cities in India. Bangalore is loved for its pleasant weather, beautiful parks and lakes all around the town. The city itself has beautifully combined its rich cultural heritage with its technological and industrial accomplishments, the synthesis of which gives the city it's unique cosmopolitan identity.
